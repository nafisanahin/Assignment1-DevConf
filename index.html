Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.

Where is DevConf 2026 taking place?

The conference will be held at a downtown venue, with full address details sent after registration.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, full refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. After that, tickets are transferable to another attendee.

Are recordings included with my ticket?

Standard tickets include 30 days of session recordings. Pro tickets get lifetime access to all recordings.

Is there a student discount?

Yes, students get 40% off the Standard ticket with a valid student ID at checkout.
